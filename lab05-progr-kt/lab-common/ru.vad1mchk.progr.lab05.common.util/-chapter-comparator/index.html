class ChapterComparator(locale: Locale) : Comparator<Chapter>
Content copied to clipboard

Compares two instances of Chapter by their fields in order: name, parent legion, and marines count. Null values go first. The string comparison is based on the java.text.Collator functionality and depends on the given locale.
